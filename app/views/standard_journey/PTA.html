<h1 class="govuk-heading-xl govuk-!-margin-bottom-8">
Your voluntary contribution options
</h1>

<p>Your options on this page are based on you continuing to contribute National Insurance until
<p><a href="national-insurance-contributions">Change the date you intend to stop paying or getting National Insurance contributions</a></p>
</p>
<p class="govuk-body">
Your current State Pension estimate is
<strong>£93.75 a week</strong>.
</p>
<p class="govuk-body">
The most you can increase your forecast to is <strong>£203.85 a&nbsp;week</strong>.
</p>
</div>
<div class="govuk-grid-column-two-thirds">
<div class="govuk-!-margin-bottom-8 govuk-!-margin-top-4">
<h2 class="govuk-heading-l">Your options</h2>
<p>You have <strong>7 options</strong> to choose from. Each option has an expiry date for payment.</p>
<p>Your options have been sorted from the lowest total cost to the highest.</p>

<h3 class="govuk-heading-s">How your options are calculated</h3>
<ul class="govuk-list govuk-list--bullet">
<li>Your options are made up of only gaps that will increase your State Pension, and the cheapest payable gaps from your National Insurance record.</li>
<li>The current tax year is not included. All filled gaps contribute equally to your State Pension, regardless of cost.</li>
<li>You have <strong>2 gaps</strong> that will not increase your State Pension. We have not included
these in your available options.</li>
<li>When more than one gap has the same cost, we will select the oldest gap first.</li>
<li><a class="govuk-link govuk-link--no-visited-state" href="contact-fpc">Contact the Future Pension Centre to find out how future years can increase your State Pension</a>.
</li>
</ul>
</div>
