Dear Research Participant ,

You can get your State Pension on 6 April 2032
Your forecast is
£124.50 a week

Your estimate:

• is not a guarantee and is based on the current law
• does not include any increase due to inflation
• does not include any years with gaps in your National Insurance record.

State Pension is usually paid every 4 weeks in arrears.

When you reach State Pension age on 6 April 2032, you no longer pay National Insurance contributions.

This date may be different if you are self-employed. For more information, go to
www.gov.uk/tax-national-insurance-after-state-pension-age/stopping-paying-
national-insurance

Your forecast may be different if there are any changes to your National Insurance information. There is more about this in the leaflet we have sent you with this letter.
