You can increase your forecast

The following years are undecided on your National Insurance record:

2017-2018
2018-2019
2019-2020

This means there are gaps that have not been included in your State Pension forecast.

You can contact HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) to find out why there are undecided
years on your record. HMRC can tell you whether you can pay for these gaps and how
much it will cost.

Call HMRC on 0300 200 3500 or textphone 0300 200 3519. If you live outside of the UK
call +44 191 203 7010.

For more information on gaps in your National Insurance record, go to
www.gov.uk/check-national-insurance-record

Your State Pension age

You will reach State Pension age on 6 April 2032. State Pension age may change in the future.
