How to pay for gaps in your National Insurance record

You can contact His Majesty's Revenue and Customes (HMRC) to find out how to pay for the
gaps in your National Insurance record and how much it will cost.

Call HMRC on 0300 200 3500 or textphone 0300 200 3519. If you live outside of the UK, call
+44 191 203 7010.

Or go to www.gov.ukand search Contact HMRC

What you can do next

Your State Pension estimate is provided for your information only. When planning for your
retirement, you should consider seeking professional advice.

The Money Helper service offers free money and pensions guidance. To find out more go
to www.moneyhelper.org.uk or call 0800 138 7777.
