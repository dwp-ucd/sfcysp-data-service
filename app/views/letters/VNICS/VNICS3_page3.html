What you can do next

Your State Pension estimate is provided for your information only. When planning for your
retirement, you should consider seeking professional advice.

The Money Helper service offers free money and pensions guidance. To find out more go
to www.moneyhelper.org.uk or call 0800 138 7777.

Your State Pension estimate is based on your National Insurance record at the time you
contacted us. Your estimate may be different if there are any changes to your National
Insurance information.

Yours sincerely

Newcastle Pension Centre, Futures Group

Treating people fairly

We are committed to the Equality Act 2010 and treating people fairly. To find out more
about this law, search ‘Equality’ on www.gov.uk

Call charges

Calls to 0800 numbers are free from personal mobiles and landlines.

Why DWP needs personal information and how we treat it

We treat personal information carefully. We may use it for any of our purposes. To learn more
about information rights and how we use information, please see our DWP Personal
Information Charter at www.gov.uk/dwp/personal information charter
