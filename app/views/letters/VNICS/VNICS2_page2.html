Undecided Years

The following years are undecided on your National Insurance record:

2018-2019
2019-2020
2020-2021

This means these years have not been included in your State Pension estimate.

You can contact His Majesty’s Revenue and Customs (HMRC) to find out why there are
undecided years on your record. HMRC can tell you whether you can pay for these years
and how much it will cost.

Call HMRC on 0300 200 3500 or textphone 0300 200 3519. If you live outside of the UK, call
+44 191 203 7010.

Or go to www.gov.uk and search Contact HMRC

If you can pay for the undecided years, you could increase your State Pension estimate.

What you can do next

Your State Pension estimate is provided for your information only. When planning for
your retirement, you should consider seeking professional advice.

The Money Helper service offers free money and pensions guidance. To find out more go
to www.moneyhelper.org.uk or call 0800 138 7777.

Your State Pension estimate is based on your National Insurance record at the time you
contacted us. Your estimate may be different if there are any changes to your National
Insurance information.
